Title: Genshin Impact

Category: Action RPG

Platform: Windows

Status: Live

In Dragon Blood, a free-to-play browser MMORPG from 101XP, you’ll harness your unique power and the blood of dragons that flows through your veins to triumph over the forces of evil scourging the land. After emerging from a thousand-year sleep, you’ll embark on your epic journey as either an intrepid warrior or powerful mage and assemble a powerful army to fight the enemy! Combat in Dragon Blood is turn-based, similar to many old school console RPGs. As you level, you’ll gain more abilities that you can use to take down your enemies. There are hundreds of different ways to build your character, and thousands of options to tailor your appearance to match your unique style. You’ll also recruit several heroes to join your party, who will bring their own talents and skills to the battlefield. You’ll need to properly manage your party’s composition, to bring the best warriors into combat and make strategic decisions to succeed against waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Dragon Blood offers plenty of content, including hundreds of quests, dungeons with challenging bosses, and weekly events, quests, and promotions. As a reward for your efforts, you’ll increase your characters’ capabilities and receive several different types of loot, up to and including fantastic dragons you can tame and have fight along your side. There are also a large number of mounts and other combat pets to acquire, so you’ll always have something new to strive for! You can also join up with your friends to create a guild and compete with other guilds for glory. During “Best Guild” competitions, the guild that achieves the highest rating receives prizes for its members. Other events award gifts for upgrading your mounts, winning battles, or even upgrading wedding rings. Rise to the top of the rankings and make your mark as the greatest warrior in the land!
